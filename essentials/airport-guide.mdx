# Airport Guide

Zimbabwe has three international airports serving different regions of the country. This guide covers everything you need to know for smooth arrivals and departures.

## Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport (HRE)

**Location:** 15km southeast of Harare city center
**IATA Code:** HRE
**Type:** Primary international gateway

### Terminal Overview

The airport has a single terminal building handling both domestic and international flights.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Arrivals" icon="plane-arrival">
    **Ground Floor**
    - Immigration counters (visa-on-arrival available)
    - Baggage claim (2 carousels)
    - Customs inspection
    - Currency exchange bureaus
    - ATMs (Visa/Mastercard)
    - Arrivals hall with hotel desks
    - Taxi and transfer pickup area
  </Card>
  <Card title="Departures" icon="plane-departure">
    **First Floor**
    - Check-in counters (opens 3 hours before flights)
    - Immigration and security screening
    - Departure lounge with shops
    - Food court and restaurants
    - Duty-free shopping
    - Business lounge access
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Business Traveler Facilities

<Card>
**Premier Lounge (Departures)**
- Location: Airside, past security
- Access: Business/First class tickets, Priority Pass, lounge memberships
- Amenities: WiFi, refreshments, newspapers, quiet work area, power outlets
- Hours: Opens 2 hours before first departure
- Day pass: ~$35-45 USD (subject to availability)

**Business Services:**
- WiFi: Available throughout terminal (free 30 minutes, paid packages available)
- Charging stations: Limited - located near departure gates
- Meeting point: Arrivals hall has small seating area
- Printing/copying: Not readily available - use hotel business centers

**Currency Exchange:**
- Multiple bureaux de change in arrivals and departures
- Rates are typically less favorable than city banks
- USD to local currency exchanges available
- ATMs accept international Visa/Mastercard (have backup cash)
</Card>

### Getting To/From Harare Airport

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="taxi" title="Taxis & Private Transfers">
    **Airport Taxis:**
    - Official taxi rank outside arrivals
    - Negotiate fare before departure
    - Typical fare to city center: $25-35 USD
    - Journey time: 25-40 minutes (depending on traffic)

    **Private Transfer Services:**
    - Most hotels offer airport pickup (arrange in advance)
    - Executive transfers: $40-60 USD
    - Pre-booking recommended for reliability

    **Ride-Hailing:**
    - Vaya Africa operates at the airport
    - Requires local mobile data/WiFi
    - Generally cheaper than airport taxis
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="car" title="Car Rental">
    **On-site Rental Companies:**
    - Avis (Terminal building)
    - Europcar (Terminal building)
    - Budget (Terminal building)
    - Several local operators

    **Tips:**
    - Pre-book for best rates and availability
    - Collect/return at terminal
    - International driving permit recommended
    - Rental costs: $50-120/day depending on vehicle
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="hotel" title="Airport Hotels">
    **Nearby Accommodation:**
    - **Express by Holiday Inn** - Adjacent to airport, business-friendly
    - **Cresta Oasis** - 10 minutes from airport
    - **N1 Hotel Airport** - Budget-friendly option nearby

    Most airport-area hotels offer:
    - Free airport shuttles
    - Early check-in/late checkout for flight schedules
    - Day rooms for layovers
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Harare Airport Tips

<Tip>
**Arrival Tips:**
- Visa-on-arrival queues can be long - have exact USD cash ready for visa fees
- Immigration forms available on arrival - fill out completely
- Customs: Declare items over $200 value
- SIM cards available from Econet/NetOne desks in arrivals

**Departure Tips:**
- Arrive 3 hours before international flights
- Check-in closes 1 hour before departure
- Departure tax included in ticket price
- Liquid restrictions apply (100ml max in carry-on)
</Tip>

## Victoria Falls International Airport (VFA)

**Location:** 20km south of Victoria Falls town
**IATA Code:** VFA
**Type:** Modern international airport (upgraded 2016)

### Terminal Overview

Modern terminal opened in 2016 with capacity for 1.5 million passengers annually.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Arrivals" icon="plane-arrival">
    **Facilities:**
    - Modern immigration hall
    - Visa-on-arrival counter
    - Baggage claim area
    - Currency exchange
    - ATM machines
    - Tour operator desks
    - Hotel booking assistance
    - Taxi and transfer area
  </Card>
  <Card title="Departures" icon="plane-departure">
    **Facilities:**
    - Spacious check-in hall
    - Efficient security screening
    - Modern departure lounge
    - Duty-free shopping
    - Restaurant and cafes
    - Craft market (airside)
    - Business lounge
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Business Traveler Facilities

<Card>
**Shongwe Lookout Lounge**
- Location: Airside, departure area
- Access: Business class, airline partners, Priority Pass, paid entry
- Amenities: WiFi, snacks, beverages, comfortable seating
- Views: Overlooks runway and bush
- Day pass: ~$30-40 USD

**Business Services:**
- WiFi: Free throughout terminal
- Charging stations: Multiple locations in departure lounge
- The terminal is modern with good facilities for working
- Limited printing services - use hotel business centers

**Important Notes:**
- Terminal is compact and easy to navigate
- Shops close when no departures scheduled
- Card payments widely accepted
</Card>

### Getting To/From Victoria Falls Airport

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="taxi" title="Transfers & Taxis">
    **Airport Transfers:**
    - Most Victoria Falls hotels include airport transfers
    - Confirm with your accommodation when booking
    - Shared shuttles available for budget travelers

    **Taxis:**
    - Available outside arrivals
    - Fixed rate to Victoria Falls town: $25-35 USD
    - Journey time: 20-25 minutes
    - Negotiate fare before departure

    **Tour Operator Transfers:**
    - Activity operators often include airport pickup
    - Confirm when booking activities
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="car" title="Car Rental">
    **On-site Rental:**
    - Avis, Budget, Europcar have desks in terminal
    - Limited selection - book in advance
    - Not essential if staying in Victoria Falls town (walkable)

    **When You Need a Car:**
    - Visiting Hwange National Park independently
    - Self-drive safari plans
    - Day trips to Botswana/Zambia borders
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Victoria Falls Airport Tips

<Tip>
**Arrival Tips:**
- Modern, efficient airport - processing is usually quick
- KAZA UniVisa available for Zimbabwe/Zambia travel ($50)
- Book activities before arrival - popular ones sell out
- Most hotels provide detailed transfer instructions

**Departure Tips:**
- Arrive 2.5 hours before international flights
- Good craft shopping in departure lounge
- Restaurant prices reasonable for airport
- Last chance to spend Zimbabwe dollars (limited use outside country)
</Tip>

## Joshua Mqabuko Nkomo International Airport (BUQ)

**Location:** 22km north of Bulawayo city center
**IATA Code:** BUQ
**Type:** Secondary international airport

### Terminal Overview

Smaller airport primarily serving regional flights and limited international connections.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Facilities" icon="building">
    **Terminal Services:**
    - Combined arrivals/departures
    - Immigration counters
    - Small baggage claim area
    - Basic refreshment facilities
    - Currency exchange (limited hours)
    - ATM machine
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connections" icon="route">
    **Flight Routes:**
    - Johannesburg (main international link)
    - Harare (domestic)
    - Limited charter flights
    - Airlines: Airlink, Fastjet
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Business Traveler Facilities

<Card>
**Limited Business Services:**
- WiFi: Available but can be slow
- No dedicated business lounge
- Basic seating areas for working
- Power outlets limited

**Tips for Business Travelers:**
- Bulawayo is mainly for industrial/mining business
- Most business travel to Bulawayo transits via Harare or Johannesburg
- Consider driving from Harare (5-6 hours) if schedules don't align
- Hotel business centers in Bulawayo well-equipped
</Card>

### Getting To/From Bulawayo Airport

<Card>
**Transfer Options:**
- Hotel transfers: Most Bulawayo hotels offer pickup (arrange in advance)
- Taxis: Available but limited - pre-arrange for reliability
- Typical fare to city center: $20-30 USD
- Journey time: 25-35 minutes

**Car Rental:**
- Limited on-site options
- Better to arrange through Bulawayo city agencies
- Useful for visiting Matobo Hills
</Card>

## Connecting Flights & Layovers

### Domestic Connections

<Card>
**Common Domestic Routes:**

| Route | Duration | Airlines | Frequency |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Harare - Victoria Falls | 1hr 30min | Fastjet, Air Zimbabwe | Daily |
| Harare - Bulawayo | 1hr | Fastjet, Air Zimbabwe | Daily |
| Victoria Falls - Bulawayo | 45min | Charter only | On request |
| Harare - Kariba | 1hr | Charter | On request |

**Connection Tips:**
- Allow minimum 2 hours between domestic connections
- Reconfirm domestic flights 24-48 hours before
- Domestic luggage limits often stricter (15-20kg)
- Keep valuables in carry-on for domestic transfers
</Card>

### International Connections

<Card>
**Major Hub Connections:**

**Via Johannesburg (OR Tambo - JNB):**
- Most common international connection
- Multiple daily flights to Harare and Victoria Falls
- Airlines: South African Airways, Airlink, British Airways (Comair)
- Allow 2+ hours for international connections

**Via Addis Ababa (ADD):**
- Ethiopian Airlines hub
- Good connections from Europe, Asia, Americas
- Direct flights to Harare and Victoria Falls

**Via Nairobi (NBO):**
- Kenya Airways connections
- Links to East Africa and beyond
- Flights to Harare and Victoria Falls

**Via Dubai (DXB):**
- Emirates connection via Harare
- Good for Middle East, Asia, Australasia travelers
</Card>

## Tips for All Zimbabwe Airports

<Card>
**General Advice:**
- Arrive 3 hours early for international flights (2.5 hours Victoria Falls)
- Have visa fees in exact USD cash ($30-55 depending on nationality)
- Keep immigration form copies - may be checked on departure
- Photography inside terminals generally prohibited
- Dress code: No specific requirements but smart casual recommended for lounge access
- Power sockets: UK-style 3-pin plugs
- Mobile SIM cards available in arrivals halls

**For Business Travelers:**
- Business lounges offer quiet work space but facilities are basic
- Download documents before arrival - WiFi can be slow
- Have a backup plan for ground transfers
- Keep emergency contacts and hotel address written down
- Business cards still commonly exchanged in Zimbabwe
</Card>
