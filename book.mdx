## Popular Zimbabwe Experiences

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wildlife Safaris" icon="paw">
    **Hwange National Park** - Zimbabwe's largest park with incredible elephant herds

    **Mana Pools** - UNESCO World Heritage Site perfect for walking safaris and canoeing

    **Gonarezhou** - Remote wilderness with spectacular landscapes and wildlife

    **Matusadona** - Lake Kariba shoreline safaris combining water and land-based game viewing
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Victoria Falls Adventures" icon="water">
    **Adrenaline Activities:**

    - White water rafting (Grade 5 rapids)
    - Bungee jumping (111m)
    - Helicopter flights over the Falls
    - Microlight flights
    - Zip-lining

    **Relaxed Experiences:**

    - Sunset cruises on the Zambezi
    - Devil's Pool swimming (low water season)
    - Rainforest walks
    - Cultural performances
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cultural Experiences" icon="users">
    **Traditional Village Visits** - Meet local communities and learn about Zimbabwean culture

    **Craft Workshops** - Stone sculpture, basket weaving, and traditional crafts

    **Heritage Sites** - Great Zimbabwe ruins, Khami ruins, Matobo Hills rock art

    **Community Tourism** - Support local initiatives while gaining authentic insights
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lake Kariba Houseboats" icon="ship">
    **Houseboat Holidays:**

    - 3-7 day expeditions on Africa's largest man-made lake
    - Tiger fishing opportunities
    - Wildlife viewing from the water
    - Swimming and water sports
    - Stargazing and sunset experiences
    - Fully catered or self-catered options

    Perfect for families, groups, or romantic getaways!
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Eastern Highlands" icon="mountain">
    **Mountain Escapes:**

    - Mt. Nyangani summit hike (Zimbabwe's highest peak)
    - Chimanimani multi-day treks
    - Tea estate tours in Honde Valley
    - Nyanga National Park exploration
    - Bvumba Mountains mist forests
    - Trout fishing in highland streams
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
