# Embassies & Consulates in Zimbabwe

Contact information for foreign diplomatic missions in Zimbabwe. Most embassies are located in Harare.

<Warning>
**Important:** Embassy details can change. Verify contact information on official embassy websites before visiting. Many require appointments for consular services.
</Warning>

---

## What Embassies Can Help With

<Card title="Services for Citizens" icon="passport">

**Emergency Services:**
- Lost/stolen passport replacement
- Emergency travel documents
- Assistance in emergencies (natural disaster, civil unrest)
- Help locating missing citizens
- Notification to family in emergencies

**Routine Consular Services:**
- Passport renewal/replacement
- Notarial services (certifying documents)
- Registration of births abroad
- Voting registration
- Citizen registration

**What Embassies Cannot Do:**
- Get you out of jail
- Pay your bills or hotel expenses
- Provide legal advice or representation
- Investigate crimes
- Get you better treatment than locals
- Intervene in commercial disputes
</Card>

## Before You Travel

<Card title="Pre-Trip Checklist" icon="list-check">

**Register Your Travel:**
- **US:** [STEP Program](https://step.state.gov)
- **UK:** [gov.uk travel registration](https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/zimbabwe)
- **Australia:** [Smartraveller](https://www.smartraveller.gov.au)
- **Canada:** [Registration of Canadians Abroad](https://travel.gc.ca/travelling/registration)

**Save These Before Departure:**
- Embassy address and phone
- Emergency contact number
- Your passport number (photo it)
- Insurance policy number
- Family contact in home country

**Make Copies:**
- Keep digital copies of passport in email/cloud
- Leave copy with family at home
- Carry separate copy from passport
</Card>
