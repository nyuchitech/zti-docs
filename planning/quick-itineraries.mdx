## 3-Day Itineraries {#3-day}

### Victoria Falls Express

<Card title="3 Days: Victoria Falls Highlights" icon="water">

**Best For:** Short trips, adding Zimbabwe to a regional itinerary
**Budget:** $500-1,500 depending on activities and accommodation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Day 1: Arrival & Orientation">
    **Morning/Afternoon:**
    - Arrive Victoria Falls Airport (VFA)
    - Transfer to hotel (20-25 minutes)
    - Check-in and freshen up

    **Evening:**
    - Sunset cruise on the Zambezi (2 hours, ~$55-75)
    - Welcome dinner at The Lookout Cafe or hotel

    **Accommodation:** Victoria Falls town
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 2: The Falls & Activities">
    **Morning:**
    - Early visit to Victoria Falls (avoid crowds, best light)
    - Walk rainforest trails (2-3 hours)
    - Breakfast/brunch in town

    **Afternoon (choose 1-2):**
    - Helicopter flight "Flight of Angels" (~$170-300)
    - Zip line/canopy tour (~$60-80)
    - Bridge tour (~$50)

    **Evening:**
    - The Boma dinner experience (~$65) OR
    - Fine dining at Livingstone Room

    **Accommodation:** Victoria Falls town
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 3: Adventure or Departure">
    **Morning (if time allows):**
    - White water rafting half-day (~$150) OR
    - Zambezi National Park game drive (~$80-120) OR
    - Craft market shopping

    **Afternoon:**
    - Transfer to airport
    - Depart

    **Alternatives if late flight:**
    - Visit Zambia side of falls (need visa/KAZA)
    - Additional activities
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Tips:**
- Book activities in advance during peak season
- KAZA UniVisa ($50) allows Zambia day trip
- Morning is best for falls photography
</Card>

## 5-Day Itineraries {#5-day}

### Victoria Falls + Hwange Taster

<Card title="5 Days: Falls & Safari" icon="paw">

**Best For:** First-time visitors wanting both highlights
**Budget:** $1,500-4,000 depending on lodge choice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Day 1: Arrive Victoria Falls">
    **Afternoon:**
    - Arrive VFA, transfer to hotel
    - Relax and orientation

    **Evening:**
    - Sunset cruise on Zambezi
    - Dinner in town
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 2: Victoria Falls Exploration">
    **Morning:**
    - Victoria Falls rainforest walk
    - Helicopter flight

    **Afternoon:**
    - Activity of choice (bridge, zip line, etc.)
    - Town exploration and crafts

    **Evening:**
    - The Boma or fine dining
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 3: Transfer to Hwange">
    **Morning:**
    - Early road transfer to Hwange (~2-2.5 hours)
    - Arrive safari lodge, check-in, lunch

    **Afternoon:**
    - First game drive (3-4 hours)
    - Sundowner drinks in the bush

    **Evening:**
    - Dinner at lodge
    - Optional night drive or stargazing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 4: Full Safari Day">
    **Early Morning:**
    - Dawn game drive (6am start)
    - Breakfast in bush or back at lodge

    **Midday:**
    - Rest at lodge, pool time
    - Lunch

    **Afternoon:**
    - Afternoon game drive (4-7pm)
    - Sundowners watching sunset

    **Evening:**
    - Dinner at lodge
    - Night drive (if offered)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 5: Final Safari & Departure">
    **Early Morning:**
    - Final game drive
    - Breakfast at lodge

    **Late Morning:**
    - Transfer to Victoria Falls Airport
    - Depart (~1pm flight or later)
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Tips:**
- Choose lodge based on budget (Main Camp area has most options)
- Road transfer is scenic; flights available but add cost
- July-October best for wildlife
</Card>

## 7-Day Itineraries {#7-day}

### Classic Zimbabwe

<Card title="7 Days: The Complete Introduction" icon="star">

**Best For:** Comprehensive first visit
**Budget:** $2,500-6,000+

<Steps>
  <Step title="Day 1: Arrive Victoria Falls">
    - Arrive VFA, transfer to hotel
    - Evening sunset cruise
    - Welcome dinner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 2: Victoria Falls Day">
    - Morning: Falls exploration, rainforest walk
    - Afternoon: Helicopter flight + 1 activity
    - Evening: Boma dinner or fine dining
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 3: Adventure Day">
    - Full-day white water rafting OR
    - Morning: Zambezi National Park; Afternoon: Activities
    - Evening: Pack for safari
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 4: Transfer to Hwange">
    - Morning transfer to Hwange (2-2.5 hrs)
    - Arrive lodge, lunch
    - Afternoon game drive
    - Evening: Lodge dinner, night drive
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 5: Full Safari Day">
    - Dawn game drive
    - Midday rest
    - Afternoon game drive
    - Sundowners, dinner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 6: Safari & Walking">
    - Morning walking safari or game drive
    - Midday: Optional cultural visit
    - Afternoon game drive
    - Farewell bush dinner (some lodges)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 7: Departure">
    - Early morning game drive (time permitting)
    - Breakfast
    - Transfer to Victoria Falls Airport
    - Depart
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Variations:**
- Add Chobe (Botswana) day trip on Day 3
- Replace Hwange days with Matobo Hills for culture/rhinos
- Extend to 8 days for more safari time
</Card>

### Victoria Falls + Great Zimbabwe

<Card title="7 Days: History & Nature" icon="landmark">

**Best For:** History buffs, cultural travelers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Days 1-3: Victoria Falls">
    As above (falls, activities, adventure)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 4: Fly to Harare, Drive to Masvingo">
    - Morning flight Vic Falls to Harare (1.5 hrs)
    - Rental car or transfer to Masvingo (3.5 hrs)
    - Evening: Arrive Great Zimbabwe area
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 5: Great Zimbabwe">
    - Full day exploring Great Zimbabwe ruins
    - Guided tour with local expert
    - Hill Complex, Great Enclosure, Valley Ruins
    - Museum visit
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 6: Lake Mutirikwi & Drive North">
    - Morning: Lake Mutirikwi activities OR
    - Drive toward Harare with stops
    - Afternoon: Arrive Harare
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 7: Harare & Departure">
    - Morning: National Gallery, city tour
    - Afternoon: Depart from Harare
  </Step>
</Steps>
</Card>

## 10-14 Day Itineraries {#10-14-day}

### Ultimate Zimbabwe Safari

<Card title="10-12 Days: Safari Circuit" icon="binoculars">

**Best For:** Safari enthusiasts, wildlife photographers
**Budget:** $5,000-15,000+

<Steps>
  <Step title="Days 1-3: Victoria Falls">
    - Day 1: Arrive, sunset cruise
    - Day 2: Falls exploration, helicopter
    - Day 3: Adventure day (rafting/activities)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Days 4-6: Hwange National Park">
    - Day 4: Transfer, afternoon drive
    - Day 5: Full safari day (AM/PM drives)
    - Day 6: Walking safari, more game drives
  </Step>

  <Step title="Days 7-9: Mana Pools">
    - Day 7: Fly/transfer to Mana Pools
    - Day 8: Walking safaris, canoe safari
    - Day 9: More walking, wildlife viewing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Days 10-11: Lake Kariba (Optional)">
    - Houseboat or Matusadona
    - Fishing, relaxation, wildlife
  </Step>

  <Step title="Day 12: Departure">
    - Fly from Kariba to Harare
    - International departure
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Tips:**
- Mana Pools best May-October (closed in rains)
- Charter flights make this routing efficient
- Can extend any section based on interest
</Card>

### Grand Zimbabwe Tour

<Card title="14 Days: Comprehensive Journey" icon="map">

**Best For:** Extended holidays, seeing everything
**Budget:** $6,000-20,000+

| Days | Location | Highlights |
|------|----------|------------|
| 1-3 | Victoria Falls | Falls, activities, adventure |
| 4-6 | Hwange | Safari, elephants, Big 5 |
| 7-8 | Matobo Hills | Rhinos, rock art, history |
| 9 | Bulawayo | City, railway museum |
| 10 | Great Zimbabwe | Ancient ruins, UNESCO site |
| 11-12 | Eastern Highlands | Mountains, hiking, scenery |
| 13-14 | Harare | Culture, galleries, departure |

**Route:** Victoria Falls → Hwange → Bulawayo → Matobo → Masvingo → Mutare/Nyanga → Harare

**This covers:**
- Victoria Falls (natural wonder)
- Safari in Hwange (wildlife)
- Cultural heritage (Great Zimbabwe, Matobo)
- Scenic diversity (Eastern Highlands)
- Urban experiences (Bulawayo, Harare)
</Card>

<Card>

| Days | Location | Why |
|------|----------|-----|

## Itinerary Customization Tips

<Card title="Building Your Own Itinerary" icon="wrench">

**Pace Guidelines:**
- 2-3 nights minimum per location
- Don't try to see everything
- Include rest/transit days
- Less is more for enjoyment

**Must-Know Distances:**

| Route | Distance | Time |
|-------|----------|------|
| Vic Falls - Hwange | 180km | 2-2.5 hrs |
| Vic Falls - Harare | 880km | 10-12 hrs (fly: 1.5 hrs) |
| Harare - Masvingo | 290km | 3.5-4 hrs |
| Bulawayo - Matobo | 35km | 45 min |
| Harare - Nyanga | 270km | 3.5-4 hrs |

**Best Combinations:**
- Victoria Falls + Hwange (natural pairing)
- Victoria Falls + Chobe, Botswana (easy addition)
- Bulawayo + Matobo (quick trip)
- Harare + Great Zimbabwe (heritage focus)
- Harare + Eastern Highlands (scenic)
</Card>
