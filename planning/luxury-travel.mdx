## When to Travel in Luxury

### Seasonal Considerations

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Peak Season">
    **June to October**
    
    Advantages:
    - Best wildlife viewing
    - Ideal weather conditions
    - All properties operational
    
    Considerations:
    - Highest rates
    - Book 6-12 months ahead
    - Some properties fully booked
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Green Season">
    **November to April**
    
    Advantages:
    - Significant discounts (30-50%)
    - Lush landscapes
    - Excellent birding
    - Fewer tourists
    - Special offers common
    
    Considerations:
    - Some camps close
    - Occasional rain
    - Different wildlife patterns
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Shoulder Seasons">
    **May & November**
    
    Perfect Balance:
    - Good wildlife viewing
    - Pleasant weather
    - Better rates than peak
    - Excellent availability
    - Special packages offered
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question" title="Is Zimbabwe safe for luxury travelers?">
    Absolutely. Luxury properties have excellent security, and Zimbabwe is generally very safe for tourists. Properties handle all logistics and transfers professionally.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="How far in advance should I book?">
    For peak season (June-October), book 6-12 months ahead. Green season offers more flexibility. Exclusive-use bookings need even longer lead times.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="What about internet connectivity?">
    Most luxury lodges offer Wi-Fi in main areas. Some provide in-room connectivity. Speeds vary but are generally sufficient for emails and messaging.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="Can special dietary needs be accommodated?">
    Yes, luxury lodges excel at catering to all dietary requirements with advance notice. Vegan, gluten-free, kosher, and halal options are available.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
