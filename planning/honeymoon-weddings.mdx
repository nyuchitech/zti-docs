# Honeymoons & Weddings

Zimbabwe offers some of Africa's most romantic settings—from the thundering spray of Victoria Falls to intimate safari camps under star-filled skies. Whether you're planning a honeymoon, destination wedding, anniversary celebration, or simply a romantic escape, this guide covers everything you need.

**Romantic Zimbabwe:** Victoria Falls consistently ranks among the world's most romantic destinations. Combined with exclusive safari experiences and the warmth of Zimbabwean hospitality, it creates unforgettable moments for couples.
</Note>

## Destination Weddings

### Getting Married in Zimbabwe

<Card title="Legal Requirements" icon="file-contract">

**For Legal Marriage:**
- Both parties must be at least 18 years old
- Valid passports required
- Birth certificates (certified copies)
- Affidavits confirming single status
- If divorced: decree absolute
- If widowed: death certificate
- Letter of no impediment from home country
- Minimum 3 working days notice required

**Process:**
1. Submit documents to Registrar's Office
2. Pay required fees
3. Wait 3 working days minimum
4. Civil ceremony at Registrar's Office
5. Additional blessing/ceremony elsewhere if desired

**Alternative:**
Many couples marry legally at home and have a blessing ceremony in Zimbabwe. This is simpler and just as romantic.

**Working with Planners:**
Wedding planners can facilitate documentation and logistics. Highly recommended for destination weddings.
</Card>

## Budget Considerations

### Cost Ranges

<Card title="What to Budget" icon="wallet">

**Honeymoon Costs (per couple):**

| Category | Budget | Mid-Range | Luxury |
|----------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Flights** | $1,500-2,500 | $2,500-4,000 | $4,000+ |
| **7 nights accommodation** | $2,000-4,000 | $5,000-10,000 | $12,000-25,000 |
| **Activities** | $500-1,000 | $1,000-2,500 | $2,500-5,000 |
| **Dining** | $300-500 | $500-1,000 | Included |
| **Transfers** | $200-400 | $400-800 | Included |
| **Extras** | $300-500 | $500-1,000 | $1,000+ |
| **Total** | ~$5,000-8,500 | ~$10,000-18,000 | $20,000-35,000+ |

**Wedding Costs:**
- Highly variable based on guest count
- Intimate wedding (10-20 guests): $5,000-15,000
- Medium wedding (50 guests): $15,000-35,000
- Large wedding (100+ guests): $35,000+
- Does not include guest travel/accommodation
</Card>

### Saving Tips

<Card>
**Honeymoon Budget Tips:**
- Travel in shoulder season (April, November)
- Book packages vs. à la carte
- Choose fewer nights at better lodges
- Combine with wedding gift registry
- Look for honeymoon specials
- Book directly for perks

**Wedding Budget Tips:**
- Smaller guest list
- Legal ceremony at home, blessing in Zimbabwe
- Weekday weddings
- Off-season timing
- Let guests cover own travel
- All-inclusive venue packages
</Card>

## Timing Your Romance

### Best Time to Visit

<Card title="Seasonal Considerations" icon="calendar">

| Season | Weather | Falls | Safari | Wedding/Honeymoon |
|--------|---------|-------|--------|-------------------|
| **Jan-Mar** | Hot, rainy | High water | Green, babies | Avoid |
| **Apr-May** | Perfect | Peak flow | Good | Excellent |
| **Jun-Aug** | Cool, dry | Good flow | Excellent | Excellent |
| **Sep-Oct** | Hot, dry | Lower flow | Best wildlife | Good (hot) |
| **Nov** | Hot, storms start | Low | Good | Moderate |
| **Dec** | Hot, rainy | Building | Green season | Avoid |

**Best Overall:** April-August

**Romantic Perks by Season:**
- **April-May:** Lush, falls at peak, comfortable
- **June-August:** Cool evenings, clear skies, cozy
- **September:** Dramatic wildlife, dramatic light

</Card>
