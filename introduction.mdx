## Travel Responsibly

Zimbabwe's natural and cultural heritage depends on responsible tourism. When you visit, please:

- Support local communities by purchasing locally-made crafts
- Respect wildlife by maintaining safe distances and never feeding animals
- Conserve water, especially in drought-prone areas
- Follow all park rules and regulations
- Ask permission before photographing local people

Your responsible choices help preserve Zimbabwe's treasures for future generations.
