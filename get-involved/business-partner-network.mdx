## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How long does the listing approval process take?">
  Most business listings are reviewed and approved within 3-5 business days. You'll receive email updates throughout the process.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I update my listing after it's published?">
  Yes! All listings include unlimited updates. You can update your business information, photos, and descriptions at any time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is a Verified Listing?">
  A Verified Listing includes a badge that shows travelers your business has been verified by our team. This gives you priority placement in search results and enhanced credibility. Contact us to learn about the verification process.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the Promotional Package work?">
  For a $100 contribution, we create custom social media content featuring your business and insert your images into relevant destination guides across our site. This gives you exposure to travelers actively researching Zimbabwe, while helping us maintain free listings for all businesses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
  We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and USD bank transfers. Payment is processed securely.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can you help with professional photography?">
  While we don't provide photography services directly, we can recommend professional photographers in your area and provide guidelines for capturing images that perform well on our platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you work with businesses outside major tourist areas?">
  Yes! We're particularly interested in businesses in emerging destinations and lesser-known locations. Our student contributor program often highlights these areas, creating new opportunities for businesses outside traditional tourist centers.
</Accordion>
